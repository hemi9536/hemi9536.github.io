Hi, I’m Henry! I’m a senior studying Computer Science at CU Boulder, and I’m passionate about learning, building, and exploring. Over the years, I’ve developed my skills through a mix of coursework, personal projects, startup experiences, and internships. I love tackling challenges that push me to try new approaches, whether it’s designing my One Pic A Day app, experimenting with startups like PerfectHomeFinder, or learning new technologies on my own. My internship at Cognizant gave me the chance to see how software development works in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, and taught me how to contribute effectively on a team while delivering meaningful work.

Outside of coding, I love the outdoors doing activities such as climbing, hiking, and snowboarding. Living in an area such as CU Boulder makes these things super accessible and I am always trying to take advantage of it. I also enjoy playing video games, as it's a fun and easy way to stay connected with long distance friends. Some games I have been playing recently are Hollow Knight and League of Legends.

This portfolio is my space to share who I am, what I’ve built, and the skills I’ve gained along the way. Throughout my portfolio you’ll find highlights of my projects, my experiences in tech, and some insights into what drives me to excel in the field of Computer Science. My goal is to give the audience a sense of my curiosity, my work ethic, and my love for experimenting. I hope you enjoy exploring it and learning a bit more about me I’m always excited to connect with people who share similar interests or passions for building and innovating.
