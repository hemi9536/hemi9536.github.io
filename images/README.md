# images/

Place your photos here and update the `src` attributes in the HTML files to point to them.
Recommended naming convention:
- `headshot.jpg` — professional photo of yourself
- `interest.jpg` — photo representing your interests
- `activity-1.jpg`, `activity-2.jpg`, `activity-3.jpg` — extracurricular photos
- `project-1.jpg`, `project-2.jpg`, `project-3.jpg` — project screenshots or photos
